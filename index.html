Hi, I’m Meghan!

My name is Meghan Brehon, and I am currently working towards an M.Sc. in atmospheric physics at the University of Saskatchewan. I did my undergrad in engineering, obtaining a B.Sc. in engineering physics in 2022. During my engineering studies, I found that I am very interested in the dynamics of the lower atmosphere and the interplay of dynamics with pollution. For my master’s thesis, I am studying carbon monoxide (CO) in troposohere above the Indian Ocean region, in particular examining the different atmospheric processes to attempt to better understand the trends in CO in this region.

Originally from rural Saskatchewan, I am currently living in Saskatoon. When not working on my studies, I like to read, knit, and in the spring and summer months work outside in the garden.
